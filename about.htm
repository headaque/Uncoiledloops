Uncoiled Loops started with the independent releases from hEADaCHE of CEOXiME , has grown to include several artists.


Uncoiled Loops is a small label, mostly done on our off time. At this time we aren't putting out other artists, but occasionally we will work with other labels as a collaborative effort.
This is what uncoiled loops would be interested in:
  • Electronic music, not necessarily dance music
  • Something different, less likely to be classifiable; although not noise or "prog rock" style
  • Something dynamic
  • Artists who are willing to work with us, not expecting everything to be done by the label
