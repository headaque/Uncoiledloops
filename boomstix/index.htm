    • Outside of Sydney, Australia is where boomstix finds his ambience. Armed with only a microphone and a recording device, he traipses through the jungle foliage for hours, recording the massive space and life sounds for his work.
    This seems far away from the dance floor, but boomstix says not so. He states "Dancing can be a form of meditation where your mind is free to explore", and this brings a state of focus and relaxation to his music.
    When not recording, you might find boomstix performing Brazilian jiu jitsu, permaculture gardening or meditating in public places.
